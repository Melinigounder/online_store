ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

All our comfortable furniture is made with a blend of eco-friendly, creative, innovative, modern design and traditional techniques.Through conscious living, creativity, education and willpower, change can happen and will have positive effects on our planet and our lives. We are energised to share our way of life and experiences with you and hopefully share some interesting ideas, insights and knowledge together.We welcome you to Melo's Furniture, our ‘Earthly Home living it up’ and trust that you will enjoy shopping with us, be inspired by our beautiful Mother Earth and leave with a sense of expanded love for all.

WHAT WE DO

Melo's Furniture Group works in every major area of development we provide a wide array of furniture products and building assistance, and we help countries share and apply innovative knowledge and solutions to the challenges they face.
